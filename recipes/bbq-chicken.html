BBQ Shredded Chicken Tostadas

Awesome recipe if you are into sweet smoke flavour.

As a Mexican, I added a little bit of spicy flavour and I am sure you're gonna love it!

Ingredients

  • Breast Chicken
  • BBQ sauce
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Corn Tostadas
  • Dry Chilli Pepper
  • Mushrooms & Onions

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken breasts.
  2. Shred the chicken breasts to your preference.
  3. In a hot pan, throw in the onion and cook it for 5 minutes.
  4. Season the shredded chicken with salt and pepper.
  5. Throw in the chicken in the hot pan and star mixing it with the onion.
  6. Add dry chilli pepper flakes and mix.
  7. Add the bbq sauce and incorporate.
  8. Add the mushrooms.
  9. Pour some bbq-chicken in a corn tostada.

Enjoy your tostadas!
