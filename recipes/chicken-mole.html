Shredded Chicken Mole

A very common yet delicious dish of Mexico

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Mole
  • Chicken Broth
  • Sugar
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken breasts in hot water.
  2. After the chicken is cooked, shred the breasts and season it with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour some chicken broth in a cooking pot.
  4. Add sugar to the pot.
  5. Keep addin sugar and chicken broth until it gets the consistency you wish.
  6. Add the shredded chicken and mix.

You can have some red rice with this dish and corn tortillas also.
